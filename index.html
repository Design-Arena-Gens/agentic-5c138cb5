Fine-tuning & datasets

LocalTrainer supports adapter-based fine-tuning with LoRA and QLoRA. Curate datasets, stage samples, and produce domain-adapted adapters ? all on your machine.

1) Add training data

Stream or import JSONL, CSV, or text. Tag samples, redact PII, and stage them into a buffer.

TRAIN:ADD source=./data/*.jsonl tags=domain:docs redact=on
2) Prepare & fine-tune

Tokenize, split, and balance classes. Run LoRA/QLoRA with automatic mixed precision and gradient checkpointing.

TRAIN:PREPARE model=Q4_K_M lora=r=16 alpha=32 target=attn,mlp
3) Evaluate & export

Run held-out evaluation, compare baselines, and export adapters for quick swapping.

TRAIN:EVAL metrics=perplexity,exact_match
TRAIN:EXPORT_ADAPTER name=domain-adapter-v1

Developer Mode commands

Toggle in chat to try them
TRAIN:ADD

Ingest samples (JSONL/CSV/TXT). Supports tags, PII redaction, and dedupe.

TRAIN:PREPARE

Tokenize, split, and configure LoRA/QLoRA hyperparameters and target modules.

TRAIN:EVAL

Run eval on held?out data; track perplexity, EM/F1, and regression metrics.

TRAIN:EXPORT_ADAPTER

Export LoRA/QLoRA adapters for fast swapping and sharing locally.

TRAIN:LOAD_ADAPTER

Load a previously exported adapter into the active model.

TRAIN:RESET_BUFFER

Clear the staged sample buffer; does not remove datasets on disk.

TRAIN:CONFIRM_PII

Explicitly approve redacted PII samples for training use.
